1. Boggle Project

Last summer, I created a Boggle Solver Web Application that allows users to upload an image of a Boggle Boggle and immediately generates a list of word solutions! Just to give a bit of a background, Boggle is a word-finding game where users are given a grid of letters and need to find as many words in sequences of adjacent letters under a time limit.
